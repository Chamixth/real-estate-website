Description

Residence 29B offers its owner a rare balance of refinement and functionality. At 1,205 square feet, this two bedroom, two bathroom residence offers 10-foot ceilings, floor-to-ceiling windows, and sweeping views of the East River from every room. An open-layout chef's kitchen, featuring professional-grade appliances by Viking and Bosch, and loft-like living room and dining area provide the perfect setting for entertaining. The spacious master bedroom boasts an oversized walk-in closet and spa-like bathroom appointed with Blue de Savoie and Stellar White marbles. The second bedroom and windowed bathroom offer breathtaking views of the RFK and George Washington Bridges. This residence also features a stackable Bosch washer/dryer. Additional storage space available for purchase. Service abounds at the Azure which features a 24 hour doorman, cold storage, and live-in resident manager. Amenities include two recently renovated landscaped roof decks, bike room, 24 hour gym outfitted with TechnoGym equipment, game room with Wi-Fi, resident's lounge, conference/dining room with adjacent kitchen and a stunning 1,500SF playroom.
